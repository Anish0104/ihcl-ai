# The Pierre — Dining and Food & Beverage Guide

## Perrine

Perrine is The Pierre's modern American fine dining restaurant. It offers a refined, contemporary menu and serves as one of the hotel's signature food and beverage experiences. The restaurant is known for its polished setting and seasonal approach.

During warmer periods, Perrine extends service to the Rose Terrace, an outdoor dining space with views toward Central Park. This makes it especially appealing for breakfast, lunch, and elegant daytime or early evening dining.

Executive Chef Vincenzo Garofalo leads the culinary direction. He is also central to the hotel's wider private dining, event catering, and signature culinary programming.

Reservations can be made through Resy via perrinenyc.com or through the concierge team. For in-house guests, staff should offer concierge assistance if they would like help securing preferred timing.

An amenity menu is available all day for hotel guests, allowing room-based dining support beyond traditional meal periods.

## Two E Bar and Lounge

Two E Bar and Lounge can be reached directly at 212-940-8113. It is open daily, Monday through Sunday.

The space was originally The Pierre's Gentleman's Library and was later redesigned by Alexandra Champalimaud. The room combines classic Upper East Side glamour with a more intimate lounge energy, making it useful for both hotel guests and local clientele.

Live jazz takes place every Thursday, Friday, and Saturday from 8:00pm to 11:00pm. No reservations are required for jazz evenings, though staff should advise guests to arrive a little earlier if they want preferred seating.

The jazz programming typically rotates across:

- American Songbook performances, including programming such as The Modern Martinis
- Soul, Pop, and Jazz sets
- Latin Jazz sets

The beverage program features bespoke cocktails, signature mocktails, curated wines, and seasonal lite bites. When taking food orders, staff should communicate that preparation generally requires 20 to 25 minutes.

Two E is especially appropriate for guests seeking:

- Pre-dinner drinks
- A classic New York lounge experience
- Intimate live music without leaving the hotel
- Alcohol-free specialty drinks in a luxury setting

## The Rotunda

The Rotunda is one of the hotel's most iconic public spaces. It features Neoclassical trompe l'oeil murals painted by Edward Melcarth in 1967. The room is visually distinctive and often functions as both a social setting and a destination in its own right.

The Rotunda serves light bites and is also used for private events. Seasonal special programming may include holiday celebrations and limited-time themed experiences.

Because the room is heavily tied to events and seasonal activations, staff should confirm current usage when guests ask about walk-in dining or event activity.

## Room Service

Room service hours are:

- Breakfast: 6:45am to 11:00am
- Lunch and Dinner: 11:00am to 10:45pm

For in-room dining requests outside those hours, staff should explain the official service windows and offer alternatives where possible, such as guest amenities or advance planning for the next meal period.

## Private Dining

Private dining is available for:

- Corporate dinners
- Milestone celebrations
- Small weddings
- Executive or family gatherings

Inquiries should be directed to 212-838-8000. Given the hotel's event pedigree, staff should treat private dining requests as potential high-value leads and route them promptly.

## Magic Table Experience

The Magic Table Experience is a limited-engagement immersive dining event led by Chef Vincenzo. It combines 3D storytelling, interactive visuals, and culinary presentation in an experiential format.

Reservations are made through themagictable.com. Because it runs on limited engagements, staff should avoid promising availability and instead guide guests to current booking channels or concierge support.

## F&B Service Notes for Staff

- Perrine is the primary refined restaurant recommendation inside the hotel.
- Two E is the main recommendation for cocktails, evening ambiance, and live jazz.
- The Rotunda is both a heritage attraction and a flexible event or light-bite venue.
- Room service timing should be communicated precisely.
- Chef Vincenzo is a key name across restaurant, event, and culinary storytelling experiences.
