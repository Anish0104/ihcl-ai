# The Pierre — Hotel Policies

## Arrival and Departure

- Check-in time: 3:00pm
- Check-out time: 12:00pm noon

Early check-in and late check-out are based on availability, and fees may apply. Staff should avoid guaranteeing either unless confirmed by the appropriate operational team.

## Age Requirement

Guests must be 18 years of age or older and present valid government-issued identification at check-in.

For guests under 21, any alcohol in the room is removed at check-in in accordance with hotel policy.

## Occupancy

Maximum room occupancy is 2 adults per room.

Children 17 and under stay free when using existing bedding in the room of a paying adult. Staff should still confirm room suitability for the family's needs, especially if extra bedding or connecting arrangements are requested.

## Extra Beds

Extra beds are available only in select rooms.

- Charge: $50 plus tax per night
- Requirement: request in advance

Because not all room categories can accommodate an extra bed, staff should frame this as subject to room type and operational confirmation.

## Payment and Authorization

A credit card pre-authorization is required upon arrival. The authorization amount is:

- Full stay value
- Plus $200 per day for incidentals
- Maximum incidental authorization cap: $600

For cash-paying guests, policy requires:

- Full prepayment of the stay
- Plus $150 per day for incidentals

Staff should communicate these requirements clearly to avoid check-in friction.

## Facility Fee

The facility fee is $30 per day, or $34.43 with tax.

It includes:

- High-speed WiFi for up to 5 devices
- Morning coffee and tea in the lobby
- PressReader with unlimited digital newspapers and magazines
- Unlimited local and domestic calls
- Bedside Reading with digital books, audiobooks, and podcasts

## Smoking

Both smoking and non-smoking rooms are available. Guests should specify preference at the time of reservation whenever possible.

## Early Departure

Early departure incurs a fee of one night's room and tax.

## Pet Policy

Dogs are welcome subject to the following policy:

- Dogs only
- Maximum 15 pounds per dog
- Maximum 2 dogs per room
- Fee: $75 per day, per dog

Operational rules:

- Dogs must be leashed except when in the guest room
- Dogs are not allowed in restaurants or the fitness center
- A dog waiver is required at check-in
- Dogs may not be left unattended in the room

Support services:

- Pet sitting can be arranged through concierge
- Pet amenities are available on request, including a dog bed, bowls, and homemade treats

Certified Service Animals are always welcome at no charge.

## WiFi

High-speed WiFi is included through the facility fee and supports streaming-quality usage on up to 5 devices.

## Family Support

Children 17 and under stay free in existing bedding with a paying adult. Family-friendly support includes:

- Kid-friendly programming
- Access to an on-call doctor if needed

## Staff Service Guidance

- Quote check-in, check-out, and authorization figures precisely.
- Never over-promise early arrival, late departure, or extra bed availability.
- For pet stays, review all operational restrictions proactively.
- The facility fee should be explained in terms of included benefits, not as an isolated charge.
