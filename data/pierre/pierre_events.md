# The Pierre — Events, Meetings, and Celebrations

The Pierre offers approximately 18,000 square feet of event space, making it one of the Upper East Side's most important venues for luxury weddings, gala dinners, executive meetings, and hybrid events.

The dedicated meetings website is thepierremeetings.com. Wedding-specific information is available at thepierrecentralparkweddings.com.

## Grand Ballroom

The Grand Ballroom is the hotel's premier large-format event venue.

- Reception capacity: up to 500 guests
- Seated dinner capacity: up to 350 guests

It features crystal chandeliers, soaring ceilings, and ornate detailing. It is best suited for:

- Galas
- Formal dinners
- Luxury weddings
- High-profile social events
- Corporate receptions

The tone is classic, ceremonial, and unmistakably grand. When describing this space, staff should emphasize prestige and visual drama.

## Cotillion Room

The Cotillion Room is another iconic Pierre venue and one of the hotel's strongest storytelling spaces.

- Reception capacity: up to 300 guests
- Seated dinner capacity: up to 200 guests

Key features include:

- 18-foot ceilings
- Three crystal chandeliers
- A private entrance from 61st Street

The room is historically associated with Big Band dancing since the 1960s and is also widely recognized as the filming location for the Al Pacino tango scene in *Scent of a Woman*. This combination of cinematic history and classic New York glamour makes it particularly compelling for wedding sales and special event storytelling.

## The Rotunda

The Rotunda is suited to intimate, visually memorable events.

- Reception capacity: up to 80 guests
- Seated dinner capacity: up to 50 guests

Its floor-to-ceiling hand-painted murals make it ideal for:

- Cocktail receptions
- Art-focused gatherings
- Small dinners
- Celebratory private moments

It is often the right recommendation when a guest or planner wants atmosphere, heritage, and intimacy over scale.

## Meeting Rooms

The Pierre offers 6 meeting rooms designed for smaller business functions.

- Capacity range: 14 to 75 guests

These rooms support:

- Board meetings
- Executive working sessions
- Small presentations
- Private client meetings
- Multi-room conference agendas

They are equipped with modern audiovisual capabilities and flexible configurations.

## Large Conference and Hybrid Capability

For large-scale conference business, The Pierre can host gatherings from 250 to 1,000 guests depending on format and space usage.

The property also supports hybrid events through King Cole AV, with capacity for 10,000 plus virtual guests. This is important for corporate sales conversations, association meetings, and events where in-person prestige must combine with digital scale.

## Catering

All catering is handled in-house by Chef Vincenzo and his team. Menus are customized, and all dietary requirements can be accommodated. This should be emphasized in both wedding and corporate event discussions because it signals flexibility without sacrificing quality.

## Positioning Notes for Staff

- The Grand Ballroom is the recommendation for scale and grandeur.
- The Cotillion Room is the recommendation for elegant mid-size events with strong storytelling value.
- The Rotunda is the recommendation for intimate, design-rich occasions.
- The meeting rooms suit structured corporate use.
- Hybrid capability is a differentiator for modern conferences.
- In-house catering means tighter quality control and a stronger luxury service promise.
