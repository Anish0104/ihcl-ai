# amã Stays and Trails — Brand and Property Overview

amã Stays and Trails is IHCL's collection of boutique heritage properties, including plantation bungalows, colonial-era estates, and nature retreats across India. It sits within the Tata Group's IHCL portfolio and represents a more intimate, capital-light hospitality model compared with full-service hotels.

The brand is growing rapidly under IHCL's Accelerate 2030 strategy and is targeting revenue growth of more than 30 percent CAGR through expansion. The word "ama" means peace and tranquility in Sanskrit, which captures the brand's intended guest experience: personal, unhurried, rooted in place, and emotionally restorative.

## Representative Property: Serenity Bungalow, Coorg

Serenity Bungalow in Coorg is a representative amã Stays and Trails property and a strong illustration of the brand model.

Coorg, also known as Kodagu, is a hill station region in Karnataka, India. It is known for coffee and spice plantations, misty hills, cool evenings, scenic drives, and abundant wildlife.

The property itself is a restored colonial-era planter's bungalow on a working coffee estate dating to the 1920s. It has:

- 4 bedrooms
- Maximum capacity of 8 guests
- Private property rental only, offered as a full buyout
- 12 acres of coffee and cardamom plantation
- Altitude of approximately 3,500 feet

The nearest town, Madikeri, is around 15 minutes away. Bangalore is approximately 5 hours away by road, making the property suitable for long-weekend escapes and slow-travel stays.

## What Is Included

Included in the stay:

- All meals: breakfast, lunch, and dinner
- Home-cooked estate cuisine
- Estate coffee and teas throughout the day
- One guided plantation walk per day
- Bonfire evenings
- One waterfall trek
- Use of all indoor and outdoor spaces
- WiFi
- All utilities
- Property caretaker on site
- Estate manager on call

## What Is Not Included

Not included in the stay:

- Alcohol, though guests may bring their own
- Transportation to and from the property
- External excursions
- Spa treatments

## Property Features

Serenity Bungalow includes a range of features that reinforce the "private estate" experience:

- Wraparound verandah with valley views
- Original teak furniture
- Four-poster beds
- Wood-burning fireplace in the living room
- Outdoor bathtub
- Kitchen garden
- Hammocks
- Telescope
- Board games and library
- Birding spots with more than 160 species recorded
- Stargazing deck

## Booking Model

Bookings are accepted on a full-property buyout basis only. The minimum stay is 2 nights, and advance booking is required. Booking inquiries can be routed through amastaystails.com or broader IHCL property channels.

## Brand Ethos

amã Stays and Trails properties are designed to feel like staying at a well-traveled friend's home rather than checking into a conventional hotel. The experience is personal, local, slow-paced, and land-connected. Staff are typically from the surrounding region and bring strong knowledge of local food, weather, customs, routes, and practical guest needs.

The key staff positioning message is this: amã is not a hotel stay packaged small. It is a hosted private-home experience with IHCL standards, local warmth, and a strong sense of place.
