# Serenity Bungalow, Coorg — Frequently Asked Questions

## Q1. What is included in the stay?
A. The stay includes all meals, estate coffee and teas throughout the day, one guided plantation walk per day, bonfire evenings, one waterfall trek, WiFi, all utilities, use of the property's indoor and outdoor spaces, and support from a resident caretaker with the estate manager on call.

## Q2. What is not included in the stay?
A. Alcohol, transportation to and from the property, external excursions, and spa treatments are not included.

## Q3. Is Serenity Bungalow booked room by room?
A. No. The property is available only as a full private buyout, which means one booking takes the entire bungalow.

## Q4. What is the minimum stay requirement?
A. The minimum stay is 2 nights, and advance booking is required.

## Q5. How many guests can stay at the bungalow, and are day visitors allowed?
A. The maximum capacity is 8 guests across 4 bedrooms. Day visitors are not permitted; the property is reserved for booked full-buyout guests only.

## Q6. Are children welcome?
A. Yes. The bungalow works very well for families because it offers privacy, outdoor space, home-style meals, and a slower pace than a traditional hotel.

## Q7. Are pets allowed?
A. Not currently. Guests should assume pets are not permitted unless a future booking confirmation states otherwise.

## Q8. How does check-in work?
A. Caretaker Raju greets guests at the gate. Arrival before 6:00pm is recommended. For late arrivals after 8:00pm, guests should contact Estate Manager Mr. Venkatesh using the number in their booking confirmation. The gate code is shared in the booking confirmation and should not be shared further.

## Q9. What are the check-out and luggage storage options?
A. Standard check-out is 11:00am. Late check-out until 1:00pm may be possible with prior arrangement. Luggage storage is available, and early arrival requests should be discussed in advance rather than assumed.

## Q10. Is WiFi available, and what is the network name?
A. Yes. Guest WiFi is available on the network `AmaSerenity_Guest`.

## Q11. Where can guests find the WiFi password?
A. The password is listed in the welcome folder on the dining table.

## Q12. How reliable is the WiFi?
A. In general it is good enough for video calls. Streaming may buffer during heavy rain, which is normal for the area during poor weather.

## Q13. What should guests do if the WiFi drops?
A. They should go to the hallway cupboard, locate the blue router, and hold the reset button for 10 seconds. If the connection does not return, they should contact the estate manager.

## Q14. What should guests do if the power goes out at night?
A. The generator should auto-start within about 30 seconds. Guests should wait briefly, use candles or torches from the living room sideboard if needed, and call the estate manager immediately if the generator does not start. They should never attempt to restart the generator themselves.

## Q15. Is the water safe to drink, and what should guests know about hot water?
A. Yes, the UV-filtered borewell water is safe to drink. Hot water usually comes from the solar heater, but it may be cooler on cloudy mornings. Each bathroom also has a backup electric geyser with the switch outside the bathroom door, and it takes about 15 minutes to heat.

## Q16. What are the meal timings?
A. Breakfast is served from 8:00am to 10:00am, lunch from 1:00pm to 2:30pm, and dinner from 7:30pm to 9:00pm.

## Q17. Can the property accommodate dietary needs, including vegetarian and vegan meals?
A. Yes. Guests should share dietary preferences, restrictions, or allergies at check-in or the night before, and Cook Lakshmi can adapt meals accordingly.

## Q18. Can guests use the kitchen themselves?
A. The kitchen can support light snacking between meals, including fruit, biscuits, coffee, and tea. Guests should not use the gas stove unless staff are present.

## Q19. What is the alcohol policy?
A. Serenity Bungalow is a dry property, so alcohol is not sold on site. Guests may bring their own alcohol, and Lakshmi can assist with corkage service.

## Q20. When is the bonfire, and are there smoking or fire restrictions?
A. Staff light the bonfire each evening around 7:00pm. Guests should not light fires independently, and smoking is not allowed indoors.

## Q21. What wildlife might guests encounter?
A. Guests may see monkeys, deer, civets, and occasionally signs such as leopard tracks near the estate border. Wildlife is part of the landscape, so food should not be left outside.

## Q22. What should guests do if they see a large animal?
A. They should remain calm, avoid running, back away slowly, stay on marked paths, and contact staff immediately. Guests should be especially careful after dark.

## Q23. What should guests pack?
A. Guests should bring a torch, rain gear, layers for cool evenings, insect repellent, and comfortable walking shoes. Even outside monsoon season, weather can change quickly.

## Q24. What is included in the daily plantation walk?
A. One guided plantation walk per day is included in the stay. Morning is usually the best time because it is cooler, clearer, and better for birdwatching.

## Q25. Is the property good for stargazing?
A. Yes. The bungalow has a stargazing deck, and the clearest conditions are typically from October to May.

## Q26. What should guests do if someone falls sick?
A. They should contact Estate Manager Mr. Venkatesh immediately using the number in the booking confirmation. Cauvery Hospital in Madikeri is about 18 minutes away, the nearest pharmacy is about 15 minutes away, and if the estate manager cannot be reached, IHCL guest services should be called.

## Q27. Is there mobile signal for calls?
A. Mobile coverage can be patchy inside the property. Signal is usually better on the verandah, and Airtel and Jio tend to perform best. WhatsApp calls over WiFi often work better than standard mobile calls.

## Q28. How far away is the nearest ATM?
A. The nearest ATM is in Madikeri, about 15 minutes away.

## Q29. How far is Serenity Bungalow from Bangalore?
A. Bangalore is approximately 5 hours away by road, depending on traffic and stop patterns.

## Q30. What is the best time of year to visit?
A. October to May is generally best for clearer weather and outdoor experiences. June to September is monsoon season, when the estate is beautiful but much wetter and trekking is less advisable.

## Q31. Is laundry available?
A. Yes. Raju can help arrange next-day laundry service.

## Q32. Can the property host birthdays or anniversaries?
A. Yes. Special celebrations can usually be arranged with prior notice through the estate manager.

## Q33. Is there a swimming pool?
A. No. The bungalow does not have a swimming pool, although there is a natural stream near the estate boundary.

## Q34. Are there quiet hours, and does the generator support air conditioning?
A. Yes. Quiet hours begin after 10:00pm out of respect for wildlife and the estate atmosphere. The generator backup supports essential operations, including air conditioning in the rooms.

## Q35. What is the cancellation policy?
A. Guests should refer to their booking confirmation for the exact cancellation terms, because policy may vary by booking arrangement.
