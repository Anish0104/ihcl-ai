# Serenity Bungalow, Coorg — Guest Guide

This guide is the operational reference for guests and staff, especially for late-night questions or situations when team members are not immediately in front of the guest.

## Arrival

Caretaker Raju greets guests at the property gate on arrival.

- Arrival is recommended before 6:00pm
- For late arrivals after 8:00pm, guests should contact Estate Manager Mr. Venkatesh using the number in the booking confirmation
- The gate code is provided in the booking confirmation email
- The gate code must not be shared

## Departure

- Standard check-out: 11:00am
- Late check-out until 1:00pm may be possible by prior arrangement
- Luggage storage is available

## WiFi

- Network name: `AmaSerenity_Guest`
- The password is printed in the welcome folder on the dining table
- Speed is suitable for video calls
- Streaming may buffer during heavy rain

If WiFi drops:

1. Go to the hallway cupboard.
2. Locate the blue router.
3. Hold the reset button for 10 seconds.

If service does not return, guests should contact the estate manager.

## Power

The estate runs on grid power with generator backup.

- If the power cuts, the generator should auto-start within 30 seconds
- If the generator does not start, call the estate manager immediately
- Guests must not attempt to restart the generator themselves
- Candles and torches are stored in the sideboard in the living room

## Water and Hot Water

The property uses borewell water filtered through UV purification, and it is safe to drink.

Hot water details:

- Main hot water comes from a solar heater
- Water may be cooler in the morning after cloudy weather
- Each bathroom also has a backup electric geyser
- The geyser switch is located outside the bathroom door
- Heating time is about 15 minutes

## Meals

Cook Lakshmi prepares all meals.

- Breakfast: 8:00am to 10:00am
- Lunch: 1:00pm to 2:30pm
- Dinner: 7:30pm to 9:00pm

Guests should share dietary requirements either at check-in or the night before. Between meals, the kitchen can support light snacking such as fruit, biscuits, coffee, and tea.

Guests must not use the gas stove unless staff are present.

## Emergencies

Estate Manager Mr. Venkatesh is available 24/7. His contact number is listed in the booking confirmation.

Caretaker Raju lives on the property in staff quarters. Guests can knock on the green door near the kitchen if immediate on-site help is needed.

Nearby essential support:

- Nearest hospital: Cauvery Hospital, Madikeri, about 18 minutes
- Nearest pharmacy: Madikeri town, about 15 minutes
- Nearest police station: Madikeri Police Station, about 16 minutes

If guests cannot reach the estate manager, they should call the IHCL guest services number provided in the booking confirmation.

## Waste

- Dry waste and recyclables should go in the dry waste bin
- Food and compostable items should go in the wet waste bin
- Guests must not litter on the plantation because wildlife is present

## Wildlife Safety

Guests may encounter monkeys, deer, civets, and occasionally leopard tracks near the estate border.

Wildlife guidance:

- Do not feed wildlife
- Do not leave food outside
- Stay on marked paths after dark
- If a large animal is encountered, remain calm
- Do not run
- Back away slowly
- Call staff immediately

## Alcohol Policy

The property is dry, which means alcohol is not sold on site.

- Guests may bring their own alcohol
- Alcohol can be purchased in Madikeri wine shops
- Corkage service is available from Lakshmi

## Fire and Smoking

- Bonfires are lit by staff each evening around 7:00pm
- Guests must not light fires independently
- Smoking is not allowed indoors

## Guest Support Reminders

- For urgent operational issues, the estate manager is the first call
- For immediate on-property help, Raju is the fastest physical point of contact
- For night questions, this guide should answer most essentials on WiFi, hot water, food timing, and backup systems
